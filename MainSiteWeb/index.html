Welcome to Mkhize & Sons Furniture Repairs

Mkhize & Sons Furniture Repairs began in 1995 when our founder, Joseph Mkhize, noticed how many beautiful pieces of furniture were being discarded simply because of minor damages or wear. With nothing but his grandfather's woodworking tools and a passion for preservation, Joseph started repairing furniture for friends and family in his small garage.

"Bringing Life Back to Your Furniture – One Piece at a Time"

At Mkhize & Sons Furniture Repairs, we don't just fix furniture — we restore memories. With over 20 years of experience, our team carefully repairs, restores, and revitalizes your treasured furniture pieces. Whether it's a cherished family heirloom or everyday furniture, we handle every piece with precision, care, and attention to detail. From wooden chairs and tables to sofas and cabinets, we ensure each project looks and feels as good as new. Explore our services today and let us help your furniture tell its story for generations to come.

Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction sets us apart. Every repair is performed using traditional craftsmanship combined with modern techniques. Trust Mkhize & Sons to bring durability, beauty, and life back to your furniture.
