Our Story

Mkhize & Sons Furniture Repairs is a proudly family-run workshop that started more than 20 years ago in Durban. What began as a small garage operation by Mr. Mkhize has grown into a trusted business serving multiple towns across KwaZulu-Natal.

Over the years, our sons and skilled artisans have joined the workshop, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern repair techniques.

Our Mission

To repair, restore and revive treasured furniture pieces so they can continue to serve families for generations. We believe every piece of furniture has a story — and we help keep that story alive.

Our Values

  • Quality: Every repair is done with care and attention to detail.
  • Trust: Our reputation is built on honesty and reliable service.
  • Community: We support local jobs and serve neighbouring towns.
  • Tradition: Skills passed down through generations.

Meet the Team

We are more than just a workshop — we are a family. Mr. Mkhize leads with decades of experience, while his sons and apprentices bring fresh energy and modern techniques. Together, we handle everything from chair repairs to full wood restorations.

Where We Work

From our main branch in Durban, we have expanded to serve customers in:

  • Durban (Main Branch)
  • Pietermaritzburg
  • Richards Bay
  • Port Shepstone
