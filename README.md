# WEDPOEPart3

Mkhize & Sons Furniture Repairs Website 

Project Overview 

A responsive website for Mkhize & Sons Furniture Repairs, showcasing 20+ years of furniture restoration expertise in KwaZulu-Natal. This website features a professional design with military green and gold color scheme, comprehensive service information, and interactive functionality. 

Company Objectives 

Business Objectives 

Establish Mkhize & Sons as the premier furniture repair service in KwaZulu-Natal 

Increase service enquiries by 40% within 6 months through online presence 

Expand service area awareness beyond current geographic reach 

Build trust through transparent pricing and quality craftsmanship demonstration 

Preserve traditional woodworking skills while integrating modern techniques 

Website Objectives 

Provide 24/7 online quoting and enquiry system for customer convenience 

Showcase restoration expertise through comprehensive gallery and case studies 

Generate 15-20 qualified leads monthly through optimized contact forms 

Educate customers on furniture repair processes and benefits 

Build brand authority through professional design and user experience 

Optimize for local search visibility in Durban and surrounding areas 

Customer Service Objectives 

Reduce pre-service questions by 60% through comprehensive information 

Set clear expectations about repair timelines and pricing 

Provide multiple contact channels (form, phone, email, locations) 

Offer transparent service explanations and cost estimations 

Maintain 24-hour response time for all customer enquiries
